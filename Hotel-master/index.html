Welcome to

Hotel Bhagyalaxmi

in Mount Road, Talheti, Abu Road, Rajasthan! Nestled amidst the serene landscapes of Abu Road, Hotel Bhagyalaxmi invites you to experience a perfect blend of comfort, elegance, and warm hospitality. Located in the heart of Rajasthan.

Our hotel offers a tranquil retreat for both business and leisure travelers. Step into our inviting lobby, where our friendly staff awaits to cater to your every need. As you make your way to our thoughtfully designed rooms, you’ll discover a sanctuary of relaxation and rejuvenation. Each room is tastefully appointed with modern amenities, ensuring a comfortable stay that exceeds your expectations.
